About Kevin

Dr. Kevin Paul Charles is a former Human Resource Manager and freelancing as the Corporate Trainer for most of the top Corporate Firms and esteemed Educational Institutions across India over a decade. He has trained over thousands of corporate professionals and over lakhs of freshmen in his career. He has also mentored and groomed hundreds of entrepreneurs and has guided them towards success.

Dr. Paul is the driving force for The Grace Academy. He is a dynamic professional who always finds it challenging to solve complex business problems. He takes high - stakes decision using his experience-backed judgment and supports it with his strong work ethics and irreproachable integrity.

In 2018, Dr. Paul took over ‘The Grace Academy’, an academy of and for Transformation in Training and Education, which imparts new age knowledge to the teachers and equips them to be leaders in the education. The academy provides expert staff to The Grace Aifa.

‎Today, he is heading the entire business of group and looks after the major arena of franchise and expansion, and hires a team to steer the organisation accordingly. Along with that, he ensures delivery of extraordinary results in growth, revenue, operational performance and profitability. He is also in charge of several upcoming dream projects.

Dr. Kevin Paul Charles is a born leader who has used his aggressiveness in the right direction. He is admired, recognized and praised for his management acumen and has transformed the level of education by his remarkable efforts. Dr. Paul has made instrumental contribution in boosting the fortunes of the organisation‎.
